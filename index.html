Welcome to Blackjack!

Rules

  • Get as close to 21 without going over.
  • If you go over 21, you bust!
  • Dealer must draw on 16 and stay on 17.
  • If dealer goes over 21, player wins!

How to play?

  • Upon entry, press the "Shuffle!" button.
  • Press the "Deal Cards!" button.
  • If you would like to draw a card, press the "Hit!" button.
  • If not, hit the "Stay!" button.
  • Hit the "New Game!" button for the next hand.
  • Don't forget to shuffle every couple hands!
